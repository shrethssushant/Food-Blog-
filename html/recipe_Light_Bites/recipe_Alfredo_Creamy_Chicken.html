Alfredo Creamy Chicken

Creamy Alfredo Chicken features tender chicken in a rich, velvety Alfredo sauce made with butter, garlic, and Parmesan. It's a comforting dish, often served over pasta for a delicious meal!

Ingredients


  • Chicken breast - 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts

  • Salt and pepper - Salt and pepper to taste

  • Olive oil - 2 tablespoons olive oil

  • Heavy cream - 1 cup heavy cream

  • Parmesan cheese - 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese

  • Garlic powder - 1 teaspoon garlic powder

  • Italian seasoning - 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning

  • Pasta - Pasta (such as fettuccine), cooked according to package instructions

Tip: Add a splash of pasta water to your sauce for extra flavor and a silky texture.

Instructions


  1. Cook the Chicken:In a large pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the seasoned chicken breasts and cook for 6-7 minutes per side until golden brown and fully cooked. Remove from the pan and set aside.

  2. Sauté Garlic:In the same pan, melt butter and add minced garlic. Sauté for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.

  3. Prepare the Sauce:Pour in heavy cream and bring to a gentle simmer. Stir in grated Parmesan cheese, salt, and black pepper. Cook for 3-4 minutes until the sauce thickens.

  4. Slice the Chicken:Slice the cooked chicken into thin strips and return it to the pan, coating it with the creamy Alfredo sauce.

  5. Combine with Pasta:Add cooked fettuccine to the pan and toss everything together until well coated.

  6. Serve:Garnish with chopped parsley and extra Parmesan cheese before serving hot.
