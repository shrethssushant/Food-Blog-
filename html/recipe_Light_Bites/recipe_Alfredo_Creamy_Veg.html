Alfredo Creamy Veg

Creamy Alfredo Veg features mushrooms in a rich, velvety Alfredo sauce made with butter, garlic, and Parmesan. It's a comforting dish, often served over pasta for a delicious meal!

Ingredients


  • Broccoli florets - 1 cup of broccoli florets

  • Unsalted butter - 2 tablespoons of unsalted butter

  • Mushrooms - 1 cup of sliced mushrooms

  • Salt and pepper - Salt and pepper to taste

  • Olive oil - 2 tablespoons olive oil

  • Heavy cream - 1 cup heavy cream

  • Parmesan cheese - 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese

  • Garlic powder - 1 teaspoon garlic powder

  • Italian seasoning - 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning

  • Pasta - Pasta (such as fettuccine), cooked according to package instructions

Tip: Add a splash of pasta water to your sauce for extra flavor and a silky texture.

Instructions


  1. Cook the Pasta:In a large pot of boiling salted water, cook the pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.

  2. Prepare the Sauce:PIn a large skillet, heat the olive oil and butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about 1 minute or until fragrant. Pour in the heavy cream and bring it to a gentle simmer. Stir in the Parmesan cheese until the sauce is smooth and creamy. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  3. Add the Vetable:Add the broccoli and mushrooms to the sauce and cook until tender, about 5-7 minutes. Stir in the spinach and cook until wilted.

  4. Combine with Pasta:Add cooked fettuccine to the pan and toss everything together until well coated.

  5. Serve:Add the cooked pasta to the skillet and toss to coat with the sauce.Garnish with chopped parsley and serve immediately.


Chef's Tip: Add cream and Parmesan for extra richness.
