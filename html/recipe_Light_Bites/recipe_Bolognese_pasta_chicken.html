Bolognese pasta Chicken

Chicken Bolognese pasta is a comforting dish featuring tender chicken in a rich, savory tomato sauce, perfectly coating the pasta for a delicious meal.

Ingredients


  • Olive oil - 2 tablespoons of olive oil

  • Onion - 1 onion, diced

  • Garlic - 2 garlic cloves, minced

  • Carrot - 2 carrots, diced

  • Ground chicken - 500g ground beef or pork

  • Celery stalks - 2 celery stalks, diced

  • Crushed tomatoes - 1 can (400g) of crushed tomatoes

  • Chicken broth - 1 cup chicken broth

  • Dried Oregano - 1 teaspoon dried oregano

  • Salt and pepper - Salt and pepper to taste

  • Spaghetti - 400g spaghetti or your choice of pasta

  • Parmesan cheese - Grated Parmesan cheese for serving

Tip: Add a splash of pasta water to your sauce for extra flavor and a silky texture.

Instructions


  1. Cook the Pasta:Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the pasta and cook according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.

  2. Saute Vegetable:In a large pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and sauté until translucent. Add the minced garlic and cook for another minute.

  3. Cook the Chicken:Increase the heat to medium-high. Add the ground chicken and cook until browned, breaking it apart with a spoon.

  4. Add Tomatoes and spices:Stir in the canned diced tomatoes, tomato paste, dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and pepper. Mix well.

  5. Simmer:Reduce the heat to low and let the sauce simmer for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally, to allow the flavors to develop.

  6. Combine:Add the cooked pasta to the sauce and toss until well coated.

  7. Serve:Serve hot with grated Parmesan cheese on top.


Chef's Tip:The secret to an irresistible Bolognese pasta is to let the sauce simmer slowly, allowing the flavors to meld beautifully and develop a rich, hearty taste.
