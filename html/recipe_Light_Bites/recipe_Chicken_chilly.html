Chicken Chilly

Chicken Chilly is a spicy Indo-Chinese dish with tender chicken and colorful peppers, tossed in a tangy, savory sauce.

Ingredients


  • Boneless chicken - 500g boneless chicken, cut into cubes

  • Corn flour - 2 tablespoons cornflour

  • All-purpose flour - 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour

  • Salt and pepper - Salt and pepper to taste

  • Soy sauce - 1 teaspoon soy sauce

  • Vegetable Oil - 3 cup for frying

  • Onion - 1 onion, diced

  • Bell pepper - 1 bell pepper, diced

  • Green Chillies - 2-3 green chilies, slit

  • Ginger-garlic paste - 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste

  • Tomato ketchup - 1 tablespoon tomato ketchup

  • Chili sauce - 1 teaspoon chili sauce (or to taste)

  • Spring onions - Spring onions, chopped (for garnish)

Tip: Keep chicken fresh by storing it in the fridge's coldest spot, wrapped tightly for deliciousness!

Instructions


  1. Marinate the Chicken:Combine chicken cubes with cornflour, all-purpose flour, salt, pepper, and a teaspoon of soy sauce. Mix well and let it marinate for about 15-20 minutes.

  2. Fry the Chicken:Heat oil in a pan and deep fry the marinated chicken until golden and crispy. Remove and drain on paper towels

  3. Prepare the Sauce:In the same pan, remove excess oil leaving about 2 tablespoons. Sauté the diced onion, bell pepper, and green chilies until slightly tender. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for another minute.

  4. Combine:Add soy sauce, tomato ketchup, and chili sauce to the pan. Stir well. Add the fried chicken to the sauce and toss until the chicken is well coated.

  5. Garnish and ServeGarnish with chopped spring onions and serve hot with rice or noodles.


Chef's Tip: Marinate chicken in soy sauce, ginger-garlic, and vinegar for a flavorful chicken chilly!
