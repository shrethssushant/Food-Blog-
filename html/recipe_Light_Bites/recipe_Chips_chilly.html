Chips chilly

"Chips Chilly"Crisp, golden potato chips infused with a fiery blend of zesty chili spices. Each bite delivers a mouthwatering crunch followed by a playful, lingering heat that satisfies any spicy snack craving. "Perfect for those who love a bold, flavorful snack adventure!"

Ingredients


  • Potatoes - 4 large, peeled and sliced into thin chips

  • Vegetable oil - 3 cups of vegetable oil for frying

  • Salt - To taste, for that essential flavor boost.

  • Red chili powder - Garlic powder, paprika, or your favorite seasoning for an extra kick.(Optional)

  • Lemon juice - 1 table spoon for that zesty flavor

  • Chilly sauce - Preferable about 1 cup for that falvour for the chips chilly

  • Vegetables - Any vegetable that you like for that crunch

Tip: Choose firm potatoes and store them in a cool, dark place to maintain freshness.

Instructions


  1. Heat Oil:In a pan, heat some oil or butter

  2. Sauté Vegetables:Add the chopped onions, garlic, and capsicum. Sauté until they are soft and fragrant.

  3. Add Sauce:Add chili sauce to the pan and mix well.

  4. Mix Chips:Add the chips to the pan and gently toss them with the sauce until they are well coated.

  5. SeasonAdd salt and pepper according to your taste.

  6. GarnishTurn off the heat and garnish with chopped spring onions or coriander leaves.

  7. ServeServe immediately and enjoy your spicy chips chili as a snack or side dish!

Chef's Tip:For an extra flavor boost, sprinkle with garlic powder or paprika right after frying for a delicious twist!
