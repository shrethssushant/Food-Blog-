Kavya Aloo

"Kavya Aloo"Crisp, golden potato wedges infused with a fiery blend of timur and nepali spice jimbu. Each bite delivers a mouthwatering crunch followed by a playful, lingering heat that satisfies any spicy snack craving. "Perfect for those who love a good spice adn a adventure"

Ingredients


  • Boiled Potatoes - 4 large, half boiled potatoes cut into wedges

  • Vegetable Oil - 3 cups of vegetable oil for frying

  • Jimbu(Dried Himalayan herb) - 2 table spoon.

  • Cumin seeds - Preferable 1 teaspoon full.

  • Salt - To your taste.

  • Green Chillies - Its optional but recomended for the kick.

  • Coriander - It is also optional for garnish

Tip: Before frying your potaotes you can wash them in cold water and pat them dry to make them more crispy.

Instructions


  1. Boil the potato:In a large pot, boil the cubed potatoes until they're tender. Drain and set aside.

  2. Prepare the Seasoning:n a pan, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the cumin seeds and let them sizzle for a few seconds.

  3. Add Jimbu:Add the jimbu to the pan and sauté for a minute until fragrant.

  4. Cook the PotatoesAdd the boiled potatoes to the pan. Sprinkle turmeric powder and salt. Mix everything well, ensuring the potatoes are well-coated.

  5. Add the Green Chillies (Optional)If using, add the chopped green chilies to the pan and mix.

  6. Cook Until DoneCook the potatoes for about 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until they are slightly crispy and well-flavored.

  7. Garnish and ServeGarnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot as a side dish or with rice or roti.


Chef's Tip: For an extra flavor boost, sprinkle with garlic powder or paprika right after frying for a delicious twist!
