Popcorn Chicken

Popcorn chicken features succulent, bite-sized pieces of chicken, encased in a golden, crunchy coating. Each morsel is perfectly seasoned, delivering a delightful combination of flavors and textures that make it a favorite snack or appetizer for chicken lovers. Served on its own or with a dipping sauce, popcorn chicken is a tasty treat perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients


  • Chicken breast - 500g cut up into bite-sized pieces

  • Vegetable Oil - 3 cups of vegetable oil for frying

  • Breadcrumbs - 1 cup.

  • Egg - 2 beaten up eggs

  • Salt and pepper - To your taste.

  • Paprika - Its optional but recomended.

  • Garlic powder - 1 tablespoon

Tip: Marinate chicken in olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, and herbs for juicy flavor.

Instructions


  1. Prepare Chicken:Cut boneless chicken into bite-sized pieces.

  2. Marinate:Soak chicken pieces in buttermilk for at least an hour to make them tender

  3. Mix Coating:Combine flour, cornstarch, garlic powder, paprika, salt, and pepper in a bowl.

  4. Coat Chicken:Remove chicken from buttermilk and thoroughly coat each piece in the flour mixture.

  5. Heat Oil:Preheat oil in a fryer or deep pan to 350°F (175°C).

  6. Fry Chicken:Fry chicken pieces in batches for 4-5 minutes until golden brown and crispy.

  7. Drain & Serve:Remove from oil, drain on paper towels, and serve hot with a dipping sauce.


Chef's Tip: Double-fry for extra crunch—first low, then high!
