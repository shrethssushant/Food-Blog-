Sausage Chilly

Chilly sausage is a delectable fusion of smoky sausage flavors with a spicy kick. Perfectly seasoned with a blend of hot peppers and aromatic spices, it delivers a mouthwatering combination that warms the palate with every bite. Fried to perfectionIdeal for those who love a little heat with their meal.

Ingredients


  • Sausage - 1 pound of sausage (your choice: pork, buff or turkey)

  • Onion - 1 onion, diced

  • Bell pepper - 1 bell pepper, diced

  • Garlic - 2 cloves garlic, minced

  • Kidney beans - 1 can (15 oz) kidney beans, drained and rinsed

  • Black beans - 1 can (15 oz) black beans, drained and rinsed

  • Tomato - 1 can (15 oz) crushed tomatoes

  • Chili powder - 1 tablespoon chili powder

  • Cumin - 1 teaspoon cumin

  • Salt and pepper - Salt and pepper to taste

  • Optional topping - shredded cheese, sour cream, chopped green onions

Tip: Simmer sausages first, then brown for juiciness and crispiness.

Instructions


  1. Cook the sausage:In a large pot, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the ground sausage and cook until it’s browned, breaking it apart with a spoon.

  2. Saute Vegetable:Add the chopped onion, minced garlic, and bell pepper to the pot. Sauté for 3-4 minutes until the vegetables are softened.

  3. Add spices:Stir in chili powder, cumin, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper. Cook for another minute to let the spices bloom.

  4. Combine Ingredients:Add the diced tomatoes, kidney beans, black beans, and chicken broth into the pot. Stir everything together.

  5. Simmer:Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and let it simmer uncovered for about 20-30 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  6. Adjust seasoning:Taste and adjust seasoning if needed before serving.

  7. Serve:Serve hot, garnished with your choice of toppings like shredded cheese, sour cream, or chopped cilantro.


Chef's Tip: Use pre-cooked sausage and canned beans for fast chili.
