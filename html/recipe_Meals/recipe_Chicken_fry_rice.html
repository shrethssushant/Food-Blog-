Chicken Fry-rice

Chicken fry rice is a savory dish featuring fluffy rice stir-fried with vibrant vegetables with chicken peas, carrots, and bell peppers, seasoned with soy sauce and spices for a flavorful and satisfying meal.

Ingredients


  • Cooked rice - 3 cups of cooked rice (Day old rice)

  • Vegetable - 1 cup of mixed vegetables (e.g., peas, carrots, bell peppers)

  • Chicken - 500g of chicken diced.

  • Soy sause - 2-3 tablespoons of soy sauce

  • Sesame Oil - 1 tablespoon of sesame oil

  • Garlic - 2 cloves of garlic, minced

  • Green onions - 2 green onions, chopped

Tip: Use cold rice, high heat, and soy sauce.

Instructions


  1. Preapre the ingredients:Chop vegetables like carrots, bell peppers, peas, and spring onions

  2. Cook the rice:Boil rice until they are almost cooked, and then drain and let them cool.

  3. Heat Oil:In a pan, heat some oil and add chopped garlic and ginger.

  4. Add vegetables and chicken:Stir-fry the vegetables and chicken until they are cooked but still crisp.

  5. Add rice:Add the cooked rice to the pan and mix well with the vegetables.

  6. Season:Add soy sauce, salt, and pepper to taste. Optionally, add a little vinegar.

  7. Mix throughlt:Stir the rice and vegetables well to combine all flavors.

  8. Garnish:Top with chopped spring onions before serving


Chef's Tip: Use cold, day-old rice for the best fried rice.
