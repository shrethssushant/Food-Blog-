Kavya Ricebowl

The Kavya Rice Bowl is a delightful and flavorful meal featuring perfectly cooked rice topped with a deliciously stir-fried egg, offering a harmonious blend of textures and tastes. It's a simple yet satisfying dish that's perfect for any time of the day.

Ingredients


    Ingredients
  • Uncooked Rice - 1 cup of uncooked rice (white or brown)

  • Vegetable oil - 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil

  • Onion - 1 small onion, chopped

  • Mixed vegetable - 1 cup of mixed vegetables (such as peas, carrots, and corn)

  • Salt and pepper - Salt and pepper to taste

  • Eggs - 2 eggs

  • Optional - Optional: cooked chicken, shrimp, or tofu for added protein

  • Green onion and sesame seeds - Green onions and sesame seeds for garnish


    • Gravy Ingredients
  • Chicken or vegetable broth - 1 cup of chicken or vegetable broth

  • Soy sause - 2 tablespoons of soy sauce

  • Corn starch mixed with water - 1 tablespoon of cornstarch mixed with 2 tablespoons of water

  • Grated ginger - 1 teaspoon of grated ginger

  • Cloves of garlic - 2 cloves of garlic, minced

  • Salt and pepper - Salt and pepper to taste

Tip: Add soy sauce, sesame oil, ginger, and garlic for flavor.

Instructions


  1. Prepare the Gravy:In a small saucepan, heat a bit of oil and sauté the ginger and garlic until fragrant. Add the broth and soy sauce, bringing it to a simmer. Stir in the cornstarch mixture and cook, stirring until the gravy thickens. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Keep warm

  2. Cook the Vegetables:In a large skillet or wok, heat the vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Add the chopped onion and stir-fry until translucent. Add the mixed vegetables and stir-fry until tender. Season with salt and pepper. Add any optional protein like chicken or shrimp, and heat through.

  3. Fry the Egg:In a separate pan, fry the eggs sunny side up or to your preferred doneness.

  4. Assemble the Bowl:Serve the plain cooked rice in bowls. Top each bowl with the stir-fried vegetables and protein, then place a fried egg on top. Pour the gravy over the rice and garnish with chopped green onions and sesame seeds.


Chef's Tip: Cook eggs in a non-stick pan with butter over medium-low heat, cover, and cook until whites set for 2-3 minutes.
