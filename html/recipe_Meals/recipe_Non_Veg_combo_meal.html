Non-veg Combo meal

Butter chicken is a creamy, spiced Indian dish with tender chicken in a rich tomato sauce.

Ingredients


  • Chicken breast - 500g chicken breast, cut into cubes

  • Yogurt - 2 tablespoons butter

  • Ginger garlic paste - 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste

  • Red chili powder - 2 teaspoons red chili powder

  • Garam masala - 1 teaspoon garam masala

  • Tumeric masala - 1 teaspoon turmeric powder

  • Cumin powder - 1 teaspoon cumin powder

  • Coriander powder - 1/2 teaspoon coriander powder

  • Cup cream - 1/2 cup cream

  • Salt - Salt to taste

  • Dried fenugreek leaves - Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

  • Basmati Rice - 1 cup of basmati rice

  • Tomato puree - 1 cup tomato puree

Tip: Rinse basmati rice, soak, then cook with 1.5 parts water for 15 minutes. Let it rest, then fluff with a fork.

Instructions


  1. Marinte the chickne:In a bowl, mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric, and salt. Add chicken to the marinade and let it rest for at least an hour.

  2. Cook the chicken:Heat oil and 1 tbsp butter in a pan over medium heat. Add marinated chicken pieces and cook until they are browned and cooked through. Remove and set aside.

  3. Prepare the Butter chicken sause:In the same pan, add tomato puree and cook until it reduces slightly. Stir in cream, garam masala, and fenugreek leaves. Adjust salt if needed. Add the cooked chicken back into the pan and let it simmer for about 10 minutes.

  4. Prepare the Jeera Rice:Rinse the rice under cold water until the water runs clear. In a saucepan, heat ghee or oil over medium heat and add cumin seeds. When cumin seeds splutter, add rinsed rice and stir for a minute. Pour in water and salt, bringing it to a boil. Cover, reduce heat, and simmer until the water is absorbed and rice is cooked (about 15-20 minutes).

  5. Serve:Serve the butter chicken hot with the fragrant jeera rice. Enjoy your meal!


Chef's Tip: Marinate Chicken in spices and yogurt for richer flavor before cooking.
