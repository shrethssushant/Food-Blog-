Chicken Noodles

Chowmein is a delightful stir-fry of noodles and vibrant, crunchy vegetables, tossed in a savory soy sauce blend—a perfect, flavorful meal for any occasion.

Ingredients


  • Chowmein noodles - 200 grams of chow mein noodles

  • Vegetable oil - 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

  • Onion - 1 onion, sliced

  • Carrot - 1 carrot, julienned

  • Bell pepper - 1 bell pepper, sliced

  • chicken breast - 200 grams of chicken breast

  • Spring onions - 2-3 spring onions, chopped

  • Soy sause - 2 tablespoons soy sauce

  • Chili sause - 1 tablespoon chili sauce (optional)

  • Vinegar - 1 tablespoon vinegar

  • Salt and pepper - Salt and pepper to taste

Tip: Add soy sauce, sesame oil, ginger, and garlic for flavor.

Instructions


  1. Cook the noodles:Boil the noodles according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and rinse with cold water to stop the cooking process. Toss with a bit of oil to prevent sticking

  2. Prepare the vegetable:Heat oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat. Add the onion and stir-fry for 1-2 minutes

  3. Add other vegetable and Chicken breast:Add carrots, bell peppers, and Chicken breast. Stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until the vegetables are slightly tender but still crisp.

  4. Combine with Noodles:Add the cooked noodles to the pan. Pour in the soy sauce, chili sauce, and vinegar. Toss everything together to coat the noodles evenly with the sauce.

  5. Season:Season with salt and pepper to taste. Stir-fry for an additional 2-3 minutes until everything is well combined and heated through

  6. Finish and serve:Garnish with chopped spring onions


Chef's Tip: Stir-fry veggies quickly, mix with noodles, soy sauce, and enjoy crisp chow mein!
