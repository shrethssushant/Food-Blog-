Americano

Americano Recipe is a simple, bold coffee made by adding hot water to a shot of espresso, creating a smooth and flavorful drink similar to regular black coffee but with a richer taste. It’s perfect for those who enjoy the intensity of espresso but want a milder, more diluted brew.

Ingredients


  • Espresso – 1 shot (about 30ml)

  • Hot water – 6 oz (180ml)

Tip: Adjust the water amount for a stronger or milder Americano.

Instructions


  1. Brew espresso: Brew one shot of espresso using an espresso machine or moka pot.

  2. Add hot water: Pour hot water into the espresso, adjusting the water amount to taste.

  3. Serve: Stir and enjoy your classic Americano!

Chef's Tip: For a hotter Americano, pre-warm your mug with hot water before brewing.
