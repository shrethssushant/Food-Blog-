Café Mocha

A café mocha is a rich and indulgent coffee drink made with espresso, steamed milk, and chocolate syrup, topped with whipped cream. It's a delightful blend that satisfies both coffee and chocolate cravings!

Ingredients


  • Espresso – 1 shot (about 30ml)

  • Steamed milk – 6-8 ounces

  • Chocolate sauce – 2 tablespoons

  • Whipped cream – For topping (optional)

Tip: Use high-quality chocolate sauce for the best flavor.

Instructions


  1. Brew espresso: Brew one shot of espresso using an espresso machine or moka pot.

  2. Prepare chocolate sauce: In a large mug, mix the chocolate sauce with the hot espresso until well combined.

  3. Steam the milk: Steam the milk using a steam wand or milk frother until it’s creamy and frothy.

  4. Combine: Pour the steamed milk into the mug with the espresso and chocolate mixture.

  5. Top: Add whipped cream on top if desired.

  6. Serve: Enjoy your delicious café mocha!

Chef's Tip: Drizzle extra chocolate sauce on top for a special touch.
