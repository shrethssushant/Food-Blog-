Cappuccino

Cappuccino is a creamy, frothy coffee made with espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam perfect for a cozy pick-me-up.

Ingredients


  • Espresso – 1 shot (about 30ml)

  • Steamed milk – 1/3 cup

  • Milk foam – 1/3 cup

  • Cocoa powder or cinnamon – For garnish (optional)

Tip: Use cold milk for better foam and serve immediately for best taste.

Instructions


  1. Brew espresso: Brew one shot of espresso using an espresso machine or moka pot.

  2. Steam the milk: Steam the milk using a steam wand or milk frother until it’s creamy and frothy.

  3. Combine: Pour the espresso into a cup, add steamed milk, and top with milk foam.

  4. Garnish: Sprinkle cocoa or cinnamon on top if desired.

  5. Serve: Enjoy your perfect cappuccino!

Chef's Tip: Use freshly ground coffee beans for the best flavor and aroma.
