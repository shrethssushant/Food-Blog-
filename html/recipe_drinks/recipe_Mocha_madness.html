Mocha Madness

Mocha Madness is an indulgent coffee drink that combines rich espresso, velvety steamed milk, and decadent chocolate, topped with whipped cream and chocolate shavings. It's a delightful treat for chocolate lovers!

Ingredients


  • Espresso – 1 shot (about 30ml)

  • Steamed milk – 6-8 ounces

  • Chocolate syrup – 2 tablespoons

  • Whipped cream – For topping

  • Chocolate shavings – For garnish (optional)

Tip: Use high-quality chocolate syrup for the best flavor.

Instructions


  1. Brew espresso: Brew one shot of espresso using an espresso machine or moka pot.

  2. Mix chocolate syrup: In a large mug, combine the hot espresso with the chocolate syrup and stir until well mixed.

  3. Steam the milk: Steam the milk using a steam wand or milk frother until it’s creamy and frothy.

  4. Combine: Pour the steamed milk into the mug with the espresso and chocolate mixture.

  5. Top: Add a generous amount of whipped cream on top and sprinkle with chocolate shavings if desired.

  6. Serve: Enjoy your delicious Mocha Madness!

Chef's Tip: Drizzle extra chocolate syrup on top for a special touch.
