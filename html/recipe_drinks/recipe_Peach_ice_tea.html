Peach Ice Tea

Peach Iced Tea is a sweet, fruity, and refreshing drink that combines the bold flavor of black tea with the juicy sweetness of ripe peaches. Perfectly chilled and poured over ice, it’s the ultimate summer sipper light, crisp, and totally satisfying. Whether you're relaxing on the porch or hosting a get-together, this vibrant tea is a crowd-pleaser every time.

Ingredients


  • Fresh peaches – 3 (sliced)

  • Black tea bags – 4

  • Water – 4 cups (2 cups boiling, 2 cups cold)

  • Sugar – 1/2 cup (adjust to taste)

  • Ice cubes – For serving

  • Fresh mint leaves – Optional, for garnish

Tip: Use ripe peaches for maximum flavor, or add a splash of peach juice for extra sweetness.

Instructions


  1. Make the peach syrup: In a saucepan, combine sliced peaches, 1/2 cup water, and sugar. Simmer for 10–15 minutes, then mash slightly and strain to get a smooth syrup.

  2. Brew the tea: Steep the tea bags in 2 cups of boiling water for 5–7 minutes. Remove tea bags and let it cool slightly.

  3. Mix the iced tea: In a pitcher, combine the brewed tea, peach syrup, and 2 cups of cold water. Stir well.

  4. Chill and serve: Fill glasses with ice cubes and pour over the peach iced tea. Garnish with fresh mint leaves or peach slices if desired. Enjoy!

Chef's Tip: For a fizzy twist, swap cold water with sparkling water just before serving.
