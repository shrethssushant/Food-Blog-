Virgin Mojito

Virgin Mojito Cool, crisp, and bursting with fresh mint and lime! This fizzy, alcohol-free classic is the ultimate refresher for sunny days or laid-back vibes. Zero booze, all the flavor.

Ingredients


  • Fresh mint leaves – 12-15 leaves

  • Lime – 2 (for juice and wedges)

  • Sugar – 2 tablespoons (adjust to taste)

  • Soda water (club soda) – 2 cups, chilled

  • Ice cubes – As needed

  • Mint sprigs and lime slices – For garnish (optional)

Tip: For a fruity twist, muddle in a few berries or a splash of fruit juice like pineapple or mango.

Instructions


  1. Muddle the mint and sugar: In a sturdy glass or pitcher, add mint leaves, sugar, and juice of 1 lime. Gently muddle using a muddler or the back of a spoon to release the mint oils.

  2. Add lime wedges: Cut the second lime into wedges and add them to the mix. Lightly press to release some juice.

  3. Add ice and soda: Fill the glass or pitcher with ice cubes. Pour the chilled soda water over the top and stir well.

  4. Garnish and serve: Garnish with extra mint sprigs and lime slices. Serve immediately and enjoy your refreshing virgin mojito!

Chef's Tip: You can substitute sugar with honey or simple syrup for a smoother blend, and use sparkling lemonade for a zesty variation.
