Hot Choclate

Hot chocolate is a warm, creamy drink made with melted chocolate or cocoa powder, milk, and sugar perfect for cozy moments and sweet cravings.

Ingredients


  • Milk – 2 cups (any kind)

  • Unsweetened cocoa powder - 2 tablespoons

  • Sugar – 2 tablespoons (adjust to taste)

  • Vanilla extract – 1/4 teaspoon (optional)

  • Salt – A pinch

  • Whipped cream or marshmallows – For topping (optional)

Tip: Use whole milk for a creamier hot chocolate or add a splash of cream for extra richness.

Instructions


  1. Make a cocoa paste: In a small saucepan, whisk together cocoa powder, sugar, salt, and 2 tablespoons of the milk to form a smooth paste.

  2. Add the rest of the milk: Gradually whisk in the remaining milk until fully combined.

  3. Heat it up: Place the saucepan over medium heat and cook, stirring constantly, until the mixture is hot but not boiling.

  4. Add vanilla: Remove from heat and stir in vanilla extract, if using.

  5. Serve: Pour into a mug and top with whipped cream or marshmallows if desired. Enjoy!

Chef's Tip: For an extra chocolaty version, stir in a few pieces of chopped dark chocolate while heating.
